The HR Circle Of Excellence Awards

(HR COE AWARDS)


Tracing and tracking the brilliance spiral in exceptional individuals

who are dedicated to the profession of human resource

management.




Overview




This concept covers the inaugural HR Excellence and recognition awards as

proposed by the Institute of Human Resources (IHRM).


Human resources in Kenya play an important role in the development of

industry and the economy at large.


It is intended that all HR Excellence and Recognition Awards winners will take

home certificates and medals and where possible have their winning

presentations picked as case studies in HR learning environments in Kenya

and East Africa.



Objectives




The Excellence and Recognition Awards seek to identify individuals and

institutions that have made an invaluable contribution to the HR practice in

the Country through time and now. These have immensely contributed to

setting standards, defining norms and practising and putting HR at the right

pedestal in their institutions.


The HR Excellence and Recognition Awards shall be looking out for

exceptional commitment, ownership passion, customer experience and drive

for excellence in ensuring the people function in orgaizations delivers on its

mandate and offers excellent stakeholder service.
